What Belltower Does

Purpose-built tools for the tasks that slow schools down

Every module in Belltower targets a specific operational pain point. Together, they give administrators a clear, connected picture of how the school runs day to day.

PTO & Leave Management

Staff submit requests online. Supervisors approve or deny in one click. Balances update automatically.

  • One-click email approval — no login required
  • Real-time balance tracking with full audit trail
  • Annual allotment policies per role
  • Automated notifications and calendar export
  • Payroll-ready PTO reports

Substitute Assignment

When staff call out, coverage gaps are surfaced immediately and subs or internal staff are matched to open periods.

  • Triggered automatically by PTO approvals
  • Outside subs or internal staff coverage
  • Period-by-period coverage view
  • Double-booking conflict warnings
  • Assignment history with Excel export

Field Trip Management

Plan, staff, and track every field trip from chaperone sign-up through vehicle assignments and student attendance.

  • Chaperone assignment with student groupings
  • Driver clearance requirements (MVR-linked)
  • Drag-and-drop vehicle assignment board
  • Payment tracking with installment support
  • Export trip rosters

Carline Dismissal

Staff enter family tag numbers on any device. Students are called on a live display screen as cars arrive.

  • Live call board for display screens
  • Audio announcements for each call
  • Real-time sync across all devices
  • All-call for remaining students

School Administration

Centralize student, family, guardian, and employee records in one searchable, filterable admin panel.

  • Students, families, guardians, and staff records
  • Tokenized guardian intake forms
  • Bulk upload and data export
  • Role-based access control
  • Year-end promotion and grade retention workflow

Volunteer Compliance

Collect and track volunteer agreements, background checks, and driver clearances across your parent community.

  • Digital volunteer agreements with e-signature
  • Background check request and status tracking
  • Chaperone and driver clearance flags
  • Per-student compliance report for teachers and admins
  • PDF export of signed agreements

Licensure Tracking

Keep staff certifications and professional licenses current with expiration tracking and automated alerts.

  • License and certification records per staff member
  • Expiration alerts sent before licenses lapse
  • Admin view across all staff at a glance
  • Staff self-service license submission

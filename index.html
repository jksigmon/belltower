What Belltower Does

Purpose-built tools for the tasks that slow schools down

Every module in Belltower targets a specific operational pain point. Together, they give administrators a clear, connected picture of how the school runs day to day.

PTO & Leave Management

Staff submit requests online. Supervisors approve or deny in one click. Balances update automatically.

  • Request & approve in minutes
  • Real-time balance tracking
  • Automated email notifications
  • Calendar & ICS export
  • Payroll-ready PTO reports

Substitute Assignment

When staff call out, coverage gaps are surfaced immediately and subs are matched to open periods.

  • Triggered by PTO approvals
  • Period-by-period coverage view
  • Notifies subs and administrators
  • Tracks assignment history

Carline Dismissal

Staff enter family tag numbers on any device. Students are announced on a live display screen as cars arrive.

  • Live call board for display screens
  • Multi-campus, simultaneous sessions
  • Instant recall & undo
  • All-call for remaining students

School Administration

Centralize student, family, guardian, and employee records in one searchable, filterable admin panel.

  • Students, families & guardians
  • Staff records & bus groups
  • Bulk upload & data export
  • Role-based access control

Why Belltower

Designed for schools,
not adapted for them

Generic HR and admin tools weren't built with school workflows in mind. Belltower is, from the way PTO balances are calculated to how dismissal runs across multiple campuses.

Less coordination overhead

PTO requests, sub assignments, and dismissal all generate the right notifications automatically. Staff spend time on students, not on chasing down approvals or coverage.

Multi-campus from day one

Schools with multiple campuses can run independent carline sessions, filter reports by location, and assign staff and students to their home campus, without extra configuration.

Built to grow with you

Modules activate when you need them. Start with PTO tracking, add dismissal when you're ready. Nothing is locked behind a configuration you can't control.
